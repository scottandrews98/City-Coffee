Menu

Cappuccino

Our cappuccino's are all about balance. This means getting the right mix of steamed milk, frothed milk and espresso. We will make sure this will be a drink to remember. The drink can be enjoyed in or placed into a take away cup for on the go use.

Latte

An espresso drink that consists of espresso, steamed milk, and foam. In a latte there is a greater percentage of steamed milk than foam.

Americano

Our Americanos need to be seen to be enjoyed. A cup filled with hot water and topped with espresso shots. An americano is an alternative to a regular cup of coffee and usually is more flavorful.
